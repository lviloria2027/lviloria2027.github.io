Achievements

Bisacool Bataan Science School (BBSS) is a school that has many achievements throughout its history. Here is the summary of our school's history, administrators, facilities, and outstanding teachers who helped build what is now known as the famous Bisacool School.

History

Bisacool Bataan Science School is a research-oriented, stem-specialized science school system in Bataan, Philippines. Established in 1964, it offers free scholarship secondary courses focusing on service-oriented subjects to prepare students for a science career. Currently, all parts of Bataan have a BBSS campus. Established through Republic Act 3991, it has been in operation since 1964.

Administrators

The Board of Trustees (BOT) is the highest policy-making body in the BBSS System, while the executive committee (ComExe) is a collegial body composed of head masters from different campuses. The executive committee is chaired by the executive head master, who coordinates policy implementation. BBSS campuses are headed by members of the ComExe.
