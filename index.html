Frequently Asked Questions

Will my daughter learn new wonderful things in this school?

Yes, we ensure that your daughter gets new knowledge and learns reliable and credible learnings from our school. She may be coached by the teacher in our school and may also join competitions.

How do I know the right course to take in this school?

You could join different clubs and activities for you to know your weaknesses and strengths, and you may apply these in choosing what course to take. Another option is to join all the courses since it's free but it may take a while to choose a course so it depends on your way.

Will there be some expenses in enrolling here?

The main expense in enrolling here would be the transportation if you prefer enrolling face-to-face but you could enroll online.

Does this school give stipends to the students?

Sadly no, but we promise to embrace joyful activities, experiments, and learnings that will enrich students' knowledge and we will provide much better teaching methods.
