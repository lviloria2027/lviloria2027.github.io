Our Popular Courses

Biology Laboratory

Biology is the favorite among all the courses wherein students can learn about the ecosystem and how all living things function. Students are also encouraged to join the Biology club and have knowledge about all types of systems. Teachers also conduct experiments and activities for students to enjoy.

Mathematics

Math is a must in learning even though many students struggle, but in our school, it is one of the courses most students take. At Bisacool School, we embrace a more easier way of teaching profound math subjects. Students join competitions, and clubs, and have a high chance of competing abroad.

English

English Class is one of the most famous courses in our school since it provides learners the knowledge and ability to write an essay, poems, stories, and many more decently and more excellently. Teachers in our school coach the students who join competitions and encourage others to join English school clubs.
